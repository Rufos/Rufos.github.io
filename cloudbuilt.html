CLOUDBUILT

Coilworks

The first release of Coilworks, which would also form the basis for the later version Super Cloudbuilt. Coilworks wanted the fast-paced, action packed gameplay to contrast with a more somber story and asked me to write the plot and narration for the game.

The story follows the dreams of a soldier named Demi, whose brain is going through a fusion with neural prosthetics after being injured in a war. Depending on which path the player takes through the game, the story diverges and offers different interpretations of what has happened and will happen in the outside world.

The end result was a story that tackled fairly heavy themes in a bright, colorful setting. Several critics praised the narrative design, with Eurogamer's review emphasizing its strength of "frequently nailing the connection between metaphor, message and play".
