STELLARIS

Paradox Development Studio's first foray into the 4X genre and the outer space setting. The game's focus on exploration in a randomly generated galaxy required new approaches for scripted content and writing.

For the base game, I wrote and scripted the dialogue of the different AI personalities, which provide flavor to the game's diplomatic system. I also designed and implemented a series of diplomatic incidents which can occur between countries based on their ideology and actions, developing differently based on the player's decisions.

For the Leviathans expansion, I designed and scripted the behavior of the Wraith, a roaming monster that spawns from a pulsar. The Wraith can be a considerable challenge in the early game, but is weakened when it enters star systems with a light frequency similar to its own. The player can find clues that hint at this weakness.

Another questline I implemented for Leviathans was the Enigmatic Fortress. This is a seemingly impenetrable vault of lost information, which takes several steps to unlock. The player has several options in how to approach the Fortress, some requiring characters with certain expertise or the acquisition of rare and exotic resources.
